ALOY

Zero Dawn's Secret Weapon

A Nora Brave, Seeker and machine hunter of unparalleled skill, Aloy is the genetic clone of 21st century roboticist and engineer Dr. Elisabet Sobeck. She was created by GAIA, the governing artificial intelligence of Zero Dawn, a global terraforming system that Sobeck had designed and whose implementation she had spearheaded, which restored life to Earth after its eradication by the Faro Plague.

Aloy is curious, determined, and intent on uncovering the mysteries of her world. Unlike many who shun the use of technology, Aloy sees technology as a practical and unique tool to aid her in her quest, a belief that was born at a young age when the focus she discovered helped her save a boy from death. She frequently uses her focus to aid in hunting machines and tracking individuals. Her early use of the focus made Aloy more aware of a bigger picture outside of the Nora tribe. As she traversed the vast landscapes of this young world, she began to see All-Mother and her vestiges as what they really are: remnants of Project Zero Dawn.
